Account and Data Deletion Request

At Sigma Tech, we respect user privacy and provide users with the option to request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please send an email to:

Email: sigmatech.dev@gmail.com

Please include the following details in your email:

  • Your registered email address (if applicable)
  • Name of the app
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User account information (if applicable)
  • Any app-related preferences or stored data

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or compliance purposes where required. Any retained data will be securely stored and not used for any other purpose.

Processing Time

Account and data deletion requests are processed within 7 business days.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding account deletion, you may contact us at:

Email: sigmatech.dev@gmail.com
